Our History

The Journey That Shaped Us

2019

The Spark

Hoorain started NailArt from her bedroom in Karachi, sharing hand-painted nail designs on social media that quickly went viral across Pakistan.

2020

The Team Forms

Mufeez joined as CTO, building the first version of the NailArt platform. Ali came on board to grow the brand's digital presence.

2022

Going Nationwide

NailArt launched its product line, partnering with local suppliers to deliver premium nail care essentials across Pakistan. Rayyan led the sales expansion.

2024

Design Evolution

Tehreem joined as Design Lead, revamping the entire visual identity and launching the gallery feature loved by thousands of nail art enthusiasts.

2026

Today & Beyond

With 50,000+ clients and growing, NailArt continues to redefine beauty, creativity, and self-expression — one nail at a time.
